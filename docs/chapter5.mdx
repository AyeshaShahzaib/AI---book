## The Seeing Machines

Computer vision is a field of AI that enables computers to "see" and interpret the visual world. This chapter explores the applications and techniques used in computer vision.

## The Computer Vision Pipeline

A typical computer vision pipeline involves several stages, from image acquisition to object recognition.

## Key Concepts

*   **Computer Vision:** A field of AI that deals with how computers can be made to gain high-level understanding from digital images or videos.
*   **Image Preprocessing:** The process of enhancing the quality of an image and removing noise.
*   **Feature Extraction:** The process of identifying and extracting key features from an image.
