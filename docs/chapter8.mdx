## The Intersection of AI and Robotics

AI is playing an increasingly important role in the field of robotics, enabling robots to perform a wide range of tasks that were previously impossible. This chapter explores the synergy between AI and robotics.

## The AI-Powered Robot

AI-powered robots are able to perceive their environment, make decisions, and take actions to achieve their goals.

## Key Concepts

*   **Robotics:** A field of engineering that deals with the design, construction, operation, and application of robots.
*   **AI-Powered Robot:** A robot that is controlled by an AI system.
*   **Perception:** The ability of a robot to perceive its environment using sensors.
