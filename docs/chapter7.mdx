## The Road Ahead

Artificial intelligence is a rapidly evolving field with the potential to transform our world in countless ways. This chapter explores some of the exciting possibilities and challenges that lie ahead.

## The Future of AI

The future of AI is likely to be shaped by a number of key trends, including the development of more powerful and general-purpose AI systems, the increasing use of AI in a wide range of industries, and the growing importance of AI ethics and governance.

## Key Concepts

*   **General AI:** A type of AI that has the ability to understand or learn any intellectual task that a human being can.
*   **AI in Industry:** The application of AI to a wide range of industries, from healthcare to finance.
*   **AI Ethics:** The study of the ethical implications of AI.
