## Your AI Journey

This chapter provides a roadmap for getting started with AI, from learning the basics to building your own AI applications.

## Your AI Roadmap

There are a number of steps you can take to get started with AI, including learning the fundamentals of math and programming, exploring different AI libraries and frameworks, and working on your own projects.

## Key Concepts

*   **AI Roadmap:** A plan for getting started with AI.
*   **AI Libraries:** Collections of pre-written code that can be used to build AI applications.
*   **AI Projects:** Opportunities to apply your AI skills to real-world problems.
